<p><p>Filter paper for coffee is categorized into non-heat-sealable and heat-sealable types. Non-heat-sealable filter paper is made from various fibers, allowing for effective filtration without the need for heat activation, making it suitable for various brewing methods. In contrast, heat-sealable filter paper features a special coating that bonds when heat is applied, enhancing durability and preventing leakage during the brewing process. Both types serve distinct preferences in coffee preparation, catering to different brewing techniques and consumer demands.</p></p>
