<p><ul><li>Fully Compostable Toothbrush</li><li>Handle Compostable Toothbrush</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The compostable toothbrush market is primarily divided into two types: fully compostable toothbrushes and handle compostable toothbrushes. Fully compostable toothbrushes are designed from biodegradable materials, allowing the entire brush—including bristles and handle—to break down in composting conditions. In contrast, handle compostable toothbrushes feature a biodegradable handle while using conventional nylon or recyclable bristles. This distinction caters to varying consumer preferences for sustainability, with fully compostable options appealing to those seeking complete eco-friendliness in oral care.</p></p>
