<p><p>The High-Voltage Electric Control System for EVs comprises components managing power flow and distribution. A **DC/DC Converter** steps down high-voltage battery power to low-voltage for auxiliary systems. The **On-Board Charger (OBC)** converts AC from charging stations to DC for battery storage. A **Power Distribution Unit (PDU)** routes power to various systems. Integrated units like **DC/DC+OBC**, **DC/DC+PDU**, and **DC/DC+PDU+OBC** combine functionalities, reducing size, weight, and complexity, enhancing efficiency and cost-effectiveness in EV power management systems.</p></p>
