<p><ul><li>High Pressure Water Mist Fire Protection System</li><li>Low Pressure Water Mist Fire Protection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Water Mist Fire Protection System market consists of two main types: high-pressure and low-pressure systems. High-pressure water mist systems utilize fine water droplets generated under high pressure to effectively suppress fires by cooling the flame and reducing oxygen levels. In contrast, low-pressure systems rely on larger water droplets produced under lower pressures, providing a cooling effect over a broader area while reducing water damage. Both systems are utilized in various industries for efficient fire protection solutions with distinct operational advantages.</p></p>
