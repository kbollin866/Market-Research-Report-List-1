<p><strong>The Disc Roller Mill Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spring Loaded</li><li>Hydraulic Pressure</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Disc Roller Mill market can be categorized into two main types based on the mechanism used for material compression: spring-loaded and hydraulic pressure mills. </p><p>Spring-loaded mills utilize tensioned springs to apply pressure, allowing for flexible adjustments in grinding settings. In contrast, hydraulic pressure mills use fluid power to generate consistent and higher pressure, ensuring uniformity in particle size. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, optimizing grinding efficiency for materials in sectors like mining, agriculture, and construction.</p></p>
