<p><strong>The Automotive Starter Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Starter Motors market includes two main types: Direct Control and Electromagnetic Control. Direct Control starter motors use a mechanical linkage to engage the starter pinion with the engine's flywheel, offering simplicity and reliability. Electromagnetic Control starter motors, on the other hand, utilize solenoids to engage the pinion, providing smoother and more efficient operation. Both types are essential for starting internal combustion engines, with electromagnetic systems being more common in modern vehicles due to their advanced functionality and ease of integration.</p></p>
