<p><ul><li>Packet Filters</li><li>Stateful Inspection</li><li>Proxy Service</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hardware firewalls market encompasses three main types: packet filters, stateful inspection, and proxy services. Packet filters analyze incoming and outgoing packets based on predefined rules, allowing or blocking traffic at a basic level. Stateful inspection adds an extra layer by tracking active connections and ensuring data packets belong to legitimate sessions. Proxy services act as intermediaries, inspecting and controlling requests and responses between clients and servers, enhancing security and performance by obscuring the network's internal structure.</p></p>
