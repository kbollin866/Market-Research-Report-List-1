<p><p>The solenoid actuator market includes pneumatic and hydraulic solenoid valves, which serve distinct applications based on their operating principles. Pneumatic solenoid valves utilize compressed air to control fluid flow, making them suitable for applications requiring quick response times and lower energy consumption. In contrast, hydraulic solenoid valves operate using pressurized fluids, offering higher force and torque output, ideal for heavy-duty applications like construction and manufacturing. Both types are crucial in automating various industrial processes, enhancing efficiency and precision in operations.</p></p>
