<p><p>The thermal insulation market comprises three primary types: cellular, fibrous, and granular materials. Cellular materials, like closed-cell foam, trap air in their structure to resist heat transfer. Fibrous materials, such as fiberglass and mineral wool, consist of interconnected fibers that provide efficient insulation by creating air pockets. Granular materials, including expanded polystyrene and perlite, utilize small particles to diminish thermal conductivity. Each type offers distinct properties and applications, catering to various insulation needs in construction, industrial, and residential sectors.</p></p>
