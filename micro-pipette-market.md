<p><ul><li>With Air Cushion</li><li>Without Air Cushion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro pipette market is divided into two main types: with air cushion and without air cushion. Air cushion pipettes utilize a spring-loaded air column for accurate liquid dispensing, making them ideal for precise measurements across various volumes. In contrast, pipettes without air cushions rely on positive displacement mechanisms, which offer greater accuracy for highly viscous or volatile liquids. Each type serves specific applications within laboratories, impacting their selection based on the required precision and liquid characteristics.</p></p>
