<p><p>The radiation monitoring safety market consists of various types of detection technologies. Scintillators convert radiation into visible light, which is then measured; they are known for their high sensitivity. Silicon photomultipliers offer excellent performance in low-light conditions, making them suitable for portable applications. Avalanche photodiodes provide fast response times and are used in high-energy environments. PIN diodes, while less sensitive, offer reliable detection in various conditions. Other technologies include gas-filled detectors and thermal detectors, each serving specific monitoring needs.</p></p>
