<p><p>The transplantation preservation solutions market comprises several types, primarily including static cold storage, hypothermic perfusion preservation, and others. Hypothermic perfusion preservation enhances this by actively perfusing organs with a preservation solution to improve nutrient supply and further extend viability. Each method addresses the critical need for organ viability before transplantation.</p></p>
