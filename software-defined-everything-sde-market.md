<p><p>Software-Defined Everything (SDE) encompasses the shift towards virtualization and automation across various IT layers. In the Software-Defined Networking (SDN) market, networks are managed through software applications, enhancing flexibility and control. Software-Defined Storage (SDS) decouples storage management from hardware, allowing for scalable and efficient data storage solutions. Software-Defined Data Center (SDDC) integrates these principles, virtualizing compute, storage, and networking resources to create a fully automated and efficient data center ecosystem, optimizing resource utilization and management.</p></p>
