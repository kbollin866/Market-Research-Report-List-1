<p><p>The quilt batting market is categorized into three main types: polyester, 100% cotton, and cotton/polyester blends. Polyester batting is lightweight, resilient, and resistant to wrinkles, making it popular for quilts requiring durability. 100% cotton batting offers breathability, softness, and a natural look, appealing to traditional quilters. Cotton/poly blends combine the best of both worlds, providing the softness of cotton with the resilience of polyester. Each type caters to diverse quilting needs, influencing quilt appearance and performance.</p></p>
