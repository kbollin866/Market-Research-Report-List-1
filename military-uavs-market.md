<p><p>The military UAV market is categorized into three primary types: fixed-wing, rotary-wing, and hybrid. Fixed-wing UAVs are designed for long-range missions, offering extended flight times and endurance, making them ideal for surveillance and reconnaissance. Rotary-wing UAVs, or drones, provide vertical takeoff and landing capabilities, excelling in urban environments and for tasks requiring agility and hovering capabilities. Hybrid UAVs combine features of both types, offering versatility and adaptability for various missions, balancing endurance with maneuverability.</p></p>
