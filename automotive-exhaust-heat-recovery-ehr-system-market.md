<p><strong>The Automotive Exhaust Heat Recovery (EHR) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhaust gas Heat Recovery (EGHR)</li><li>Rankine cycle systems</li><li>Thermoelectric generator</li><li>Electric Turbo Compounding (ETC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Exhaust Heat Recovery (EHR) System market includes several technologies. Exhaust Gas Heat Recovery (EGHR) captures waste heat from exhaust gases to improve engine efficiency. Rankine cycle systems utilize this heat to generate steam, powering a turbine for additional energy. Thermoelectric generators convert temperature differences into electricity, enhancing overall vehicle efficiency. Electric Turbo Compounding (ETC) recovers energy from the exhaust to drive a generator, further optimizing performance while reducing emissions. These technologies collectively aim to enhance fuel efficiency and reduce environmental impact.</p></p>
