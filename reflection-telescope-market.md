<p><ul><li>Main Focus System</li><li>Newton System</li><li>Cassegrain System</li><li>Gregorian System</li><li>Folding Shaft System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reflection telescope market can be categorized into several main types based on optical designs. The main focus system provides direct optical alignment for minimal distortion. The Newtonian system uses a concave primary mirror and a flat secondary mirror, offering a wide field of view. The Cassegrain system combines a primary mirror with a secondary mirror, ideal for high magnification. The Gregorian system features a concave secondary mirror, enhancing image quality. The folding shaft system incorporates internal mirrors for compact designs, along with other innovative types.</p></p>
