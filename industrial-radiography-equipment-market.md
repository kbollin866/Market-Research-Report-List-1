<p><ul><li>Film-based Radiography</li><li>Digital Radiography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Radiography Equipment market comprises two primary types: film-based radiography and digital radiography. Film-based radiography utilizes traditional X-ray films to produce images of materials for inspection, offering high-resolution results but requiring chemical processing. In contrast, digital radiography employs digital sensors to capture and display images immediately, providing faster results and enhanced image analysis capabilities. Both methods are utilized for non-destructive testing (NDT) to evaluate the integrity of structures and components across various industries.</p></p>
