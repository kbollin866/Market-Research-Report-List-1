<p><strong>The Drum Liners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flexible Drum Liner</li><li>Rigid Drum Liner</li><li>Semi-rigid Drum Liner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The drum liners market is segmented into three main types: flexible drum liners, rigid drum liners, and semi-rigid drum liners. Flexible drum liners are lightweight and can conform to the drum's shape, offering ease of use and compatibility with various liquids. Rigid drum liners provide structural integrity and are suitable for hazardous materials, ensuring safety during storage and transport. Semi-rigid drum liners combine features of both, providing moderate flexibility while maintaining strength, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
