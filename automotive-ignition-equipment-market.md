<p><p>The automotive ignition equipment market primarily includes battery-operated ignition systems and magneto systems. Battery-operated ignition utilizes a battery to supply voltage, enabling reliable spark generation for combustion engines. These systems are commonly found in modern vehicles due to their efficiency and adaptability. Conversely, magneto systems generate electrical power through magnetic induction, often seen in small engines and some classic vehicles. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to diverse automotive needs and preferences in ignition technology.</p></p>
