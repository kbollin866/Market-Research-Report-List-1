<p><p>The biomedical sensors market encompasses several types, each serving distinct purposes. Pressure sensors monitor forces within the body, crucial for cardiovascular assessments. Temperature sensors track body heat, vital for fever detection and monitoring. Image sensors capture visual data for diagnostic imaging, aiding in disease detection. Motion sensors detect movement, useful for rehabilitation monitoring. Inertial sensors measure acceleration and angular velocity, supporting various medical applications. Other sensors include biosensors and chemical sensors, expanding their utility in healthcare diagnostics and patient monitoring.</p></p>
