<p><p>The pneumatic grinders market primarily includes two types: pneumatic angle grinders and pneumatic straight grinders. In contrast, pneumatic straight grinders are ideal for tasks requiring straight grinding, cutting, and finishing operations, providing a more direct application. Both types leverage compressed air for efficient power delivery and are widely used in manufacturing and automotive industries for their reliability and performance.</p></p>
