<p><p>The canola oil market comprises two primary types: cold-pressed canola oil and extracted canola oil. Cold-pressed canola oil is obtained through mechanical pressing without heat, preserving its flavor and nutritional qualities. In contrast, extracted canola oil is produced using solvents or chemical methods, allowing for higher yields and longer shelf life, but may have a different flavor profile. Both types cater to diverse consumer preferences, with cold-pressed being favored for health-oriented applications and extracted oil commonly used in food processing.</p></p>
