<p><p>Electrostatic Discharge (ESD) bags are specialized packaging materials designed to protect electronic components and devices from static electricity, which can cause damage during storage or transportation. They prevent electrostatic discharge, ensuring the safe handling of sensitive components like semiconductors, circuit boards, and integrated circuits.</p><p>The Electrostatic Discharge Bags Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period.
