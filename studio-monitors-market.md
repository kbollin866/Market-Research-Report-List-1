<p><p>The studio monitors market is primarily divided into active and passive monitors. Active monitors integrate built-in amplifiers, providing a balanced sound and simplifying setup. Both types serve professional and home recording environments, catering to diverse preferences in sound fidelity, space constraints, and budget considerations, ultimately enhancing the audio production experience.</p></p>
