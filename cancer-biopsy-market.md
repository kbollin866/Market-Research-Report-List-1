<p><p>The cancer biopsy market is predominantly categorized into tissue biopsies and liquid biopsies, along with other specialized methods. Tissue biopsies involve the extraction of a small sample of tumor tissue for histological examination, providing detailed insights into cancer types and stages. Liquid biopsies, on the other hand, analyze circulating tumor cells or DNA in bodily fluids like blood, offering a less invasive alternative for monitoring cancer progression and treatment response. Other methods may include needle aspirations or fine-needle biopsies, complementing these two primary categories.</p></p>
