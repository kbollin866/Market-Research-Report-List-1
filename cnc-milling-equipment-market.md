<p><strong>The CNC Milling Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>CNC milling equipment is categorized primarily into horizontal and vertical types, each serving distinct applications. Horizontal CNC mills feature a spindle oriented horizontally, allowing for efficient chip removal and greater stability, making them ideal for large parts and heavy material. Vertical CNC mills have a spindle aligned vertically, offering versatility for intricate designs and smaller workpieces. The choice between horizontal and vertical milling depends on factors such as project requirements, material types, and desired precision in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
