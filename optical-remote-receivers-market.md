<p><strong>The Optical Remote Receivers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Receiving Type</li><li>Passive Receiving Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical remote receivers market is categorized into two main types: active and passive receiving types. Active receiving types utilize electronic components to amplify and process incoming signals, allowing for enhanced sensitivity and range. They are ideal for applications requiring high performance in challenging environments. In contrast, passive receiving types operate without additional power, relying solely on incoming light signals. This makes them simpler and more energy-efficient, suitable for low-power applications where cost and reliability are prioritized.</p></p>
