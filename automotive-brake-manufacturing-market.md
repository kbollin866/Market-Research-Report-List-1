<p><p>The automotive brake manufacturing market includes various advanced technologies that enhance vehicle safety and performance. The Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) prevents wheel lock-up during braking, improving control. Electronic Stability Control (ESC) helps maintain vehicle stability during disruptions. Electronic Brake Distribution (EBD) optimizes brake force between front and rear wheels for better efficiency. The Traction Control System (TCS) manages wheel spin during acceleration, enhancing grip. Together, these systems contribute significantly to overall automotive safety and driving dynamics.</p></p>
