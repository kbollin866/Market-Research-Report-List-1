<p><strong>The Single-phase Gear Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid-shaft Gear Motor</li><li>Hollow-shaft Gear Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-phase gear motors market consists of two primary types: solid-shaft and hollow-shaft gear motors. Solid-shaft gear motors feature a single, rigid shaft that transmits power directly, making them ideal for applications with limited space. In contrast, hollow-shaft gear motors have a cylindrical opening that allows them to be mounted over existing shafts, facilitating easier installation and reducing wear on components. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, manufacturing, and robotics, due to their reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
