<p><p>The Cystic Fibrosis therapeutics market includes several key types of treatments. Pancreatic enzyme supplements aid digestion by providing necessary enzymes that CF patients lack. Mucolytics help thin mucus, making it easier to clear from the lungs. Bronchodilators work to relax and open the airways, improving breathing. CFTR modulators target the underlying genetic defect by enhancing the function of the cystic fibrosis transmembrane conductance regulator protein, thereby improving lung function and overall patient health. Each plays a critical role in managing CF.</p></p>
