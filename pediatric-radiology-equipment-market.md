<p><p>The Pediatric Radiology Equipment Market encompasses various imaging technologies tailored for children. Ultrasound uses sound waves for safe, radiation-free imaging, ideal for soft tissue evaluation. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) provides detailed images of organs and tissues using magnetic fields, suitable for complex diagnostics. Computed Tomography (CT) offers rapid, cross-sectional images, beneficial for emergency cases but involves radiation. X-ray imaging is widely used for bone assessment and detecting conditions. Other technologies may include nuclear medicine and fluoroscopy, catering to diverse pediatric needs.</p></p>
