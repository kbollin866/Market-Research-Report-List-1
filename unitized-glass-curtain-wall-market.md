<p><p>The Unitized Glass Curtain Wall market includes three main types: Double Glazed, Three Glazed, and Single Glazed. Double Glazed units feature two glass panes with an insulating gap, offering enhanced thermal and acoustic performance. Three Glazed units add an extra pane, further improving energy efficiency and sound insulation, ideal for extreme climates. Single Glazed units consist of one glass layer, providing basic functionality at a lower cost, suitable for mild environments. Each type caters to specific architectural and environmental needs, balancing performance, cost, and sustainability.</p></p>
