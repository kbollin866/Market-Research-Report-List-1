<p><ul><li>Parallax Barrier Technology</li><li>Lenticular Display Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The glass-free HD 3D display market primarily consists of two technologies: parallax barrier and lenticular display. Parallax barrier technology uses a layer of slits to direct light towards each eye, creating a 3D effect without glasses. In contrast, lenticular display technology employs an array of lenses to project different images to each eye based on viewing angle, enhancing depth perception. Both technologies cater to various applications, including gaming, advertising, and medical imaging, offering immersive visual experiences without the need for eyewear.</p></p>
