<p><p>The carbon steel rebars market primarily consists of deformed steel and mild steel bars. Deformed steel rebars feature ridges that enhance bonding with concrete, making them ideal for high-strength constructions. They are widely used in commercial and infrastructure projects. Mild steel rebars, known for their ductility and flexibility, are softer and easier to work with, making them suitable for residential and low-stress applications. Together, these types cater to various construction needs, balancing strength and workability.</p></p>
