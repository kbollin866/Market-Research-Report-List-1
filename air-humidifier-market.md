<p><ul><li>Vapor Type Humidifiers</li><li>Water Spray Humidifiers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air humidifier market comprises various types, including vapor type humidifiers and water spray humidifiers. Vapor type humidifiers use a heating element to produce steam, which cools before being released into the air, increasing humidity effectively. In contrast, water spray humidifiers disperse fine water droplets into the air, directly raising moisture levels without heating. Both types serve to improve indoor air quality, alleviate dryness, and provide comfort in living spaces, catering to diverse consumer preferences and health needs.</p></p>
