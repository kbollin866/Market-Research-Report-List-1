<p><p>The carpet yarn market features several types, each with unique characteristics. Nylon carpet fibers are durable and resilient, making them ideal for high-traffic areas. Polyester (PET) fibers are soft, stain-resistant, and environmentally friendly. PTT (Triexta Polyester) combines the benefits of both nylon and polyester, offering comfort and stain resistance. Polypropylene (Olefin) is moisture-resistant and suitable for outdoor use. Wool fibers provide natural warmth and luxury, while cotton offers softness and versatility, appealing to eco-conscious consumers.</p></p>
