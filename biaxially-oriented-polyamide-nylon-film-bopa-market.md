<p><p>Biaxially Oriented Polyamide (BOPA) film is a versatile packaging material known for its strength and barrier properties. The market encompasses three main types: Sequential Stretching Type, where film is stretched in one direction followed by the perpendicular direction, enhancing tensile strength; Mechanical Simultaneous Stretching Type, which stretches the film in both directions at once for improved uniformity; and LISIM Simultaneous Stretching Type, utilizing a special process for higher efficiency and superior optical properties, catering to diverse packaging applications.</p></p>
