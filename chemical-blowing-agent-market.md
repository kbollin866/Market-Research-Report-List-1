<p><p>The Chemical Blowing Agent (CBA) market includes types like HCFC, HC, HFC, and Others, used to create foam in polymers. HCFCs (Hydrochlorofluorocarbons) are ozone-depleting but effective, being phased out due to environmental concerns. HCs (Hydrocarbons) are eco-friendly, cost-effective, and widely used in insulation. HFCs (Hydrofluorocarbons) are non-ozone-depleting but have high global warming potential, facing regulatory restrictions. "Others" include emerging alternatives like water-based or nitrogen-based agents, focusing on sustainability and compliance with environmental regulations. Each type balances performance, cost, and environmental impact.</p></p>
