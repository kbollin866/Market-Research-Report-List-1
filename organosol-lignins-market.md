<p><ul><li>Ethanol/Water Pulping (Alcell Process)</li><li>Pulping With Acetic Acid (CIMV Process)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organosol lignins market encompasses two primary types based on pulping processes. The Ethanol/Water Pulping (Alcell Process) utilizes a combination of ethanol and water to dissolve lignin from biomass, resulting in high-quality lignins suitable for various applications. In contrast, the Pulping With Acetic Acid (CIMV Process) employs acetic acid for lignin extraction, offering enhanced efficiency and yield. Both processes cater to an increasing demand for sustainable and bio-based materials in industries such as adhesives, coatings, and bioplastics.</p></p>
