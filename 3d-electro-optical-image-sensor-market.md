<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The 3D Electro-optical Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stereo Vision</li><li>Structured Light</li><li>Time-of-Flight</li><li>Ultrasound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D electro-optical image sensor market comprises distinct types: </p><p>1. **Stereo Vision** captures depth using two or more cameras to mimic human binocular vision, offering precise spatial mapping. </p><p>2. **Structured Light** projects a known light pattern onto a scene and analyzes deformations to gauge depth information effectively. </p><p>3. **Time-of-Flight (ToF)** calculates distance by measuring the time light takes to return from an object, providing rapid depth data. </p><p>4. **Ultrasound** utilizes sound waves for depth measurement, often in medical and industrial applications, delivering accurate spatial readings.</p></p>
