<p><p>The liquid filter cartridges market includes various types, primarily membrane, pleated, and depth filter cartridges. Membrane filter cartridges use a semi-permeable membrane to remove particles and microorganisms from liquids, offering high filtration precision. Pleated filter cartridges maximize surface area for effective sediment and particulate removal, making them suitable for higher flow rates. Depth filter cartridges, on the other hand, consist of porous materials that trap contaminants throughout the filter's depth, providing a robust and durable filtration solution for various applications.</p></p>
