<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Radiographic Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Film Radiography</li><li>Real Time Radiography (RTR)</li><li>Computed Tomography (CT)</li><li>Digital Radiography (DR)</li><li>Computed Radiography (CR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiographic testing encompasses various techniques for inspecting materials and structures using radiation. Film radiography employs photographic film to capture images, while real-time radiography (RTR) provides immediate feedback through continuous imaging. Computed tomography (CT) offers detailed cross-sectional images by processing multiple radiographic images. Digital radiography (DR) uses digital sensors for quick image acquisition and analysis, enhancing efficiency and image quality. Computed radiography (CR) combines traditional film techniques with digital technology, using phosphor plates to capture images, facilitating easier storage and processing.</p></p>
