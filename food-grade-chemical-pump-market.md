Pneumatic pumps utilize compressed air for operation, making them ideal for environments where electricity poses a risk. Hydraulic pumps use pressurized fluid to create motion, offering high pressure and flow rates for demanding applications. Electric pumps are powered by electrical energy, providing consistent performance and ease of use. Each type serves specific needs in food processing, ensuring safety, efficiency, and compliance with hygiene standards.</p></p>
