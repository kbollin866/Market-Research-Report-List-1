<p><p>The commercial vehicle suspension system market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Passive Suspension System**: This traditional system uses fixed components to absorb shocks and provide stability, relying on springs and dampers without external control.</p><p>2. **Semi-Active Suspension System**: This advanced system adjusts damping characteristics in real-time, enhancing ride comfort and handling by responding to road conditions without fully active components.</p><p>3. **Active Suspension System**: This high-tech option employs sensors and actuators to dynamically adjust to driving conditions, offering superior performance, comfort, and vehicle stability by constantly changing suspension settings.</p></p>
