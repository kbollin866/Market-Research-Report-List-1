<p><strong>The Industrial Humidity Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Humidity Sensor</li><li>Absolute Humidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial humidity sensor market comprises two primary types: relative humidity sensors and absolute humidity sensors. Relative humidity sensors measure the moisture content in the air relative to the maximum amount it can hold at a given temperature, making them ideal for applications like HVAC and weather monitoring. In contrast, absolute humidity sensors measure the actual amount of water vapor present in the air, regardless of temperature, making them suitable for processes requiring precise moisture control, such as manufacturing and food processing.</p></p>
