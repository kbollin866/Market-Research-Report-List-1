<p><p>The PE cling film market primarily comprises three types of polyethylene: LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene), and HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene). LDPE is known for its flexibility and ease of use, making it ideal for food wrapping. LLDPE offers enhanced stretchability and strength, providing a tighter seal. HDPE, being firmer and more rigid, is less common in cling films but is used for specific applications. Each type caters to different needs in packaging and preservation.</p></p>
