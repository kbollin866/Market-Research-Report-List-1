<p><ul><li>Swinging-bucket Rotors</li><li>Fixed-angle Rotors</li><li>Vertical Rotors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solid-from-liquid centrifuge market features three primary rotor types: swinging-bucket, fixed-angle, and vertical rotors. Swinging-bucket rotors allow sample tubes to swing outward during spinning, optimizing sedimentation for larger particles. Fixed-angle rotors maintain a constant angle, providing efficient separation in less time, suitable for small-volume applications. Vertical rotors hold samples in a vertical position, maximizing separation force and reducing sedimentation time for dense samples. Each rotor type caters to specific applications in laboratories and industrial settings, enhancing process efficiency.</p></p>
