<p><p>The corrugating paperboard market consists of various types catering to different packaging needs. Single wall paperboard comprises one layer of fluted material between two liners, making it lightweight and cost-effective for basic packaging. Double wall paperboard has two layers of flutes, offering enhanced strength and cushioning for heavy or fragile items. Triple wall paperboard features three fluted layers, providing maximum durability and protection for extremely heavy or sensitive goods. Other types may include specialty boards designed for specific applications or industries.</p></p>
