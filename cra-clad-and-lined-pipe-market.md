<p><p>The CRA clad and lined pipe market consists of two primary types: CRA clad pipes and CRA lined pipes. CRA clad pipes feature a layer of corrosion-resistant alloy (CRA) bonded to a carbon steel base, providing enhanced durability and resistance to corrosive environments. In contrast, CRA lined pipes have a corrosion-resistant alloy liner within a carbon steel casing, offering similar protection while allowing for easier replacement of the liner. Both types are used in industries where corrosion presents a significant challenge.</p></p>
