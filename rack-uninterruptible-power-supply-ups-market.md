<p><p>The Rack Uninterruptible Power Supply (UPS) market is segmented into three types: Static UPS, Dynamic UPS, and Hybrid UPS. Static UPS relies on batteries and power electronics for energy storage and conversion, providing stable power during outages. Dynamic UPS utilizes flywheels or rotating systems for instant power continuity, offering a longer lifecycle. Hybrid UPS combines both static and dynamic technologies, optimizing efficiency and reliability. Each type addresses specific power needs, ensuring uninterrupted operations for critical infrastructure.</p></p>
