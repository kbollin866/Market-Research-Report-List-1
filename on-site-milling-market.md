<p><p>On-site milling encompasses various methods tailored for specific applications in construction and maintenance. Linear milling features a straight, back-and-forth motion, ideal for surface repairs and leveling in straight line segments. Gantry milling, with its overhead support frame, allows for larger work areas, making it suitable for big projects such as bridge or roadwork. Orbital milling employs a circular path, effectively addressing complex geometries and contours. Each method optimizes efficiency and precision in situ, enhancing project outcomes.</p></p>
