<p><p>The bonded Nd-Fe-B magnets market consists of two main types: injected magnets and pressed magnets. Injected magnets are produced through a process that combines polymer with magnetic powder, allowing for intricate shapes and high precision. They offer improved mechanical properties and surface finishes. Pressed magnets, on the other hand, involve compacting the magnetic powder in a mold, followed by sintering to enhance magnetic performance. Both types cater to diverse applications, including automotive, electronics, and renewable energy sectors, driven by their unique attributes.</p></p>
