<p><ul><li>Straight Through Type</li><li>In-Out Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SQF furnace market is divided into two main types: Straight Through Type and In-Out Type. The Straight Through Type allows continuous production, where materials are fed in one end and exit from the other, enhancing efficiency and throughput. Conversely, the In-Out Type features batch processing, where materials enter and exit through the same opening, allowing for controlled heating cycles and flexibility for different applications. Both types cater to varying industrial needs, optimizing energy usage and production processes.</p></p>
