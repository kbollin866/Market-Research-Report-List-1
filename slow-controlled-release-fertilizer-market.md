<p><p>The Slow & Controlled Release Fertilizer market is primarily categorized into Binary Compound Fertilizers and NPK Compound Fertilizers. Binary Compound Fertilizers contain two essential nutrients, typically a combination of nitrogen and phosphorus or potassium, designed to provide a balanced nutrient supply. In contrast, NPK Compound Fertilizers encompass a broader spectrum, incorporating nitrogen (N), phosphorus (P), and potassium (K) in varying ratios to support comprehensive plant growth. Both types enhance nutrient efficiency, reduce leaching, and support sustainable agricultural practices.</p></p>
