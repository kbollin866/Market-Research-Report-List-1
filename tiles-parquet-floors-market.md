<p><p>The Tiles Parquet Floors market is primarily divided into two types: engineered and solid. Engineered parquet floors consist of multiple layers of wood veneer over a plywood base, offering enhanced stability and moisture resistance, making them suitable for various environments. Conversely, solid parquet floors are crafted from a single piece of hardwood, providing a classic aesthetic and durability but are more susceptible to environmental changes. Both types cater to different preferences in design and functionality for flooring solutions.</p></p>
