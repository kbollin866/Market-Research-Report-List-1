<p><strong>The Bunionectomy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Osteotomy</li><li>Exostectomy</li><li>Arthrodesis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bunionectomy market consists of three primary types: osteotomy, exostectomy, and arthrodesis. Osteotomy involves cutting and repositioning the bone to correct misalignment, providing long-term relief and improved foot function. Exostectomy focuses on removing the bony bump on the big toe while maintaining joint integrity, ideal for less severe conditions. Arthrodesis involves fusion of the affected joint to eliminate pain, typically reserved for advanced cases. Each method offers distinct benefits tailored to patient needs and bunion severity.</p></p>
