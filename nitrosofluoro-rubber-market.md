<p><p>The Nitrosofluoro Rubber market is categorized primarily into two types: Carboxylnitroso and Nitroso. Carboxylnitroso rubber features carboxylic acid groups, enhancing its properties like adhesion and chemical resistance, making it suitable for automotive and industrial applications. On the other hand, standard Nitroso rubber offers exceptional thermal stability and elasticity, commonly used in seals, gaskets, and hoses. These variations cater to different industry needs, supporting high-performance applications across various sectors, including aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
