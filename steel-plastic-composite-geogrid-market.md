<p><p>The Steel-Plastic Composite Geogrid market is categorized into two primary types: biaxial tension and uniaxial tension. Biaxial tension geogrids are designed to provide reinforcement in both horizontal and vertical directions, making them suitable for various applications like road base reinforcement and soil stabilization. In contrast, uniaxial tension geogrids are optimized for load-bearing in one direction, often used in retaining walls and slopes. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the specific engineering requirements and site conditions.</p></p>
