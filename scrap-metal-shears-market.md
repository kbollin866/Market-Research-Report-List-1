<p><p>Scrap metal shears come in four main types: hydraulic, electric, pneumatic, and manual. Hydraulic shears use hydraulic power for increased force and efficiency, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Electric shears are powered by electricity, offering speed and precision for various metal cutting tasks. Pneumatic shears operate using compressed air, providing efficient cutting in industrial settings. Manual shears require physical effort, suitable for lighter tasks or smaller operations. Each type serves different needs based on power, application, and operational environment.</p></p>
