<p><p>The Silicon AFM (Atomic Force Microscope) probes market is divided into two main types: coated and uncoated probes. Coated probes feature thin layers of materials like metal or polymers, enhancing performance for specific applications, such as improving conductivity or reducing friction. Uncoated probes, on the other hand, consist solely of silicon and are primarily used for general-purpose imaging and analysis. The choice between these types depends on the particular needs of researchers and industries requiring precise surface measurements and characterizations.</p></p>
