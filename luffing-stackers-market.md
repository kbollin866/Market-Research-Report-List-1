<p><p>Luffing stackers are specialized equipment used in bulk material handling, categorized into slewing and non-slewing markets. Slewing luffing stackers have the ability to rotate around a vertical axis, allowing for greater maneuverability and efficiency in stacking materials from various angles. In contrast, non-slewing luffing stackers maintain a fixed base while adjusting the boom's angle, providing stability and simplicity in operations. Both types cater to different operational needs in industries such as ports, mining, and logistics, enhancing productivity in material management.</p></p>
