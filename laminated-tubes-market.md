<p><ul><li>Plastic Barrier Laminate (PBL)</li><li>Aluminium Barrier Laminate (ABL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laminated tubes market consists primarily of Plastic Barrier Laminate (PBL) and Aluminium Barrier Laminate (ABL) types. PBL tubes are made from multiple layers of plastic, offering flexibility and excellent moisture resistance, making them suitable for products like cosmetics and pharmaceuticals. ABL tubes, on the other hand, incorporate an aluminium layer, providing superior barrier protection against light, air, and moisture, ideal for sensitive products such as ointments and food items. Both types cater to diverse packaging needs while ensuring product integrity.</p></p>
