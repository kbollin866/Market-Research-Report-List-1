<p><strong>The Latte Instant Coffee Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spray-drying</li><li>Freeze-drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The latte instant coffee market encompasses two primary types: spray-drying and freeze-drying. Spray-drying involves rapidly evaporating water from brewed coffee through hot air, resulting in fine coffee powder. This method is cost-effective and preserves flavor to some extent. In contrast, freeze-drying involves freezing brewed coffee and then removing moisture under a vacuum, which maintains more flavor and aroma characteristics. Both methods cater to consumer preferences for convenience while impacting cost, quality, and market positioning.</p></p>
