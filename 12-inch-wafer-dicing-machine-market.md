<p><p>The 12-inch wafer dicing machine market comprises two primary types: grinding wheel dicing machines and laser dicing machines. Grinding wheel dicing machines utilize abrasive blades to precisely cut silicon wafers into smaller pieces, making them suitable for high-volume production with cost-effectiveness. In contrast, laser dicing machines employ focused laser beams for more intricate cuts, allowing for minimal material loss and handling delicate semiconductor materials without mechanical stress. Both types cater to the evolving needs of the semiconductor industry.</p></p>
