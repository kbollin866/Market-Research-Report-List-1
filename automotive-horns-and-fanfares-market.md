<p><p>The automotive horns and fanfares market includes various types of horns designed for vehicles. **Air horns** use compressed air to produce loud, deep tones, ideal for large vehicles like trucks. **Electronic horns** rely on electronic circuits to generate sound, offering compact designs and moderate volume, suitable for passenger cars. **Electromagnetic horns** use electromagnetic coils and diaphragms to create sharp, high-pitched sounds, commonly found in motorcycles and smaller vehicles. Each type caters to specific vehicle needs, balancing sound intensity, size, and application.</p></p>
