<p><p>Submerged Arc Welding (SAW) is a high-efficiency industrial welding process where an arc is formed between a continuously fed electrode and the workpiece, with the weld area being submerged beneath a granular flux. This method offers deep penetration and minimal spatter, making it ideal for thick steel plates and large structures. The market for submerged arc welding is witnessing robust growth due to an increase in demand from the construction, shipbuilding, and energy sectors.</p><p>Factors driving the market include the need for automation in welding processes, the rise in infrastructure development, and advancements in welding technology. The growing emphasis on renewable energy projects, particularly in wind and solar industries, is also contributing to market expansion. The Submerged Arc Welding Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.4% during the forecast period. Additionally, innovations in welding consumables and flux materials are enhancing efficiency and performance, further propelling the market.
